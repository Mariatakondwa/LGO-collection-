THE LGO STORY

Resilience • Culture • Statement Pieces

"Life Goes On. No matter the hustle, the block, or the obstacles—we keep moving."

Born and bred in the heart of Midrand, LGO COLLECTION (Life Goes On) is more than just a clothing label—it’s a mindset. Co-founded by lifelong best friends Maria and Ayabonga during their final matric year, the brand was sparked by a shared obsession with raw urban culture, kasi pride, and high-end streetwear.

What started as sketches in the back of high school notebooks has evolved into a bold cultural movement. Maria and Ayabonga set out to create premium garments that empower youth and represent the resilient, unstoppable spirit of their community. Every drop features signature heavy-weight fabrics, sharp typography, and authentic local flair designed for maximum impact on the streets.

As LGO expands across South Africa, our mission remains clear: to push the boundaries of local fashion and elevate authentic kasi streetwear to the global stage. Stay resilient. Stay stylish.
